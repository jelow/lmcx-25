Lower Mainland Cyclocross is a grassroots cycling organization committed to building an inclusive, accessible, and exciting cyclocross community across the Vancouver area. Our mission is to spark enthusiasm for the sport by supporting riders of all ages and skill levels, uniting local race organizers, and promoting events through clear, centralized communication.
In 2025, we’re proud to relaunch our little kids race series, KinderCross! This is an initiative designed to introduce young riders—especially those from underrepresented age groups and communities—to the joy of cyclocross in a fun, welcoming environment. By fostering connection, participation, and diversity within the sport, Lower Mainland Cyclocross aims to make cycling more than just a race—it’s a community.
